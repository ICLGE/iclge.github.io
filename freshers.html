Welcome to ICLGE!


We run both casual and competitive events throughout the year for a wide variety of games, as well as social events.

We participate in external league tournaments run by National Student Esports (NSE) and the National University Esports League (NUEL). Competitions run weekly for games including League of Legends, Valorant, CS:GO, Overwatch, Dota 2, Rainbow 6 Siege and more. We also host online and LAN matches in-house and against other universities for our teams to compete in.
